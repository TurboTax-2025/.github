## What is TurboTax?

**TurboTax** is a leading tax preparation software developed by Intuit, designed to simplify the process of filing income tax returns for individuals and small businesses in the United States and Canada. It transforms the complex, often stressful task of tax filing into a straightforward, guided interview.

With TurboTax, users can confidently navigate various tax situations, from simple W-2s to more complex scenarios involving investments, rental properties, and self-employment income. The software asks simple questions and uses your answers to fill in the appropriate tax forms, ensuring you claim all eligible deductions and credits to maximize your refund or minimize your tax liability. Its robust error-checking and audit risk meter provide an added layer of security, making it a trusted choice for millions of filers each year. Whether you're a first-time filer or have intricate financial details, TurboTax offers a tailored experience to meet your needs.
